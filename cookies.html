1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information to the website owners. Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, Finops Experts) are called “first-party cookies.” Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called “third-party cookies.”

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We may use both “session” cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and “persistent” cookies (which stay on your device until you delete them or they expire). Below are some categories of cookies that may be used on our Site:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for our Site to function properly and cannot be switched off without impairing the Site’s performance. They are usually only set in response to your actions, such as filling out forms or setting your privacy preferences.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our Site, such as which pages are visited most often and if any errors occur. We use these cookies to improve the functionality and user experience of our Site.
  • Functionality Cookies: These enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering choices you make (like language preferences). If you do not allow these cookies, some services may not function properly.
  • Targeting and Advertising Cookies: These cookies may be set through our Site by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other websites. They do not store directly personal information but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and device.

5. Managing and Disabling Cookies

Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can typically modify your browser settings to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or alert you when new cookies are being sent. How you do this varies from browser to browser. Consult the “Help” or “Settings” menu of your browser for instructions.
